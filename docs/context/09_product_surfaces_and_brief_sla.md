# 09 — Product Surfaces and Brief SLA

## Home base

The embedded Shopify App Bridge app is the home base.

Why:
- App Store distribution
- Built for Shopify later
- evidence review
- diffs
- previews
- approval moments
- positioning as a destination with an agenda

Chat surfaces cannot carry the trust-critical review moments alone.

## Push heartbeat

Pull-only engagement kills this category.

The daily brief should travel to the merchant at 7am via:
- email
- Slack
- WhatsApp later where appropriate

The brief deep-links back into the embedded app for:
- evidence
- approvals
- previews
- diffs
- feedback

## Brief uptime is a product SLA

A manager that goes silent for two days breaks the habit and trust.

The daily brief needs:
- monitoring
- job status
- retry path
- degraded mode
- incident comms

Silence is not acceptable.

## Degraded mode

If data is delayed, send a degraded brief:

> Data is delayed. Here is what I can verify.

Never pretend certainty when data is incomplete.

## Sidekick extension

Build a Sidekick app extension behind a flag once extensions are generally available.

Treat Shopify's surface as:
- a possible channel
- not a dependency
