# 15 — Company, Money and Metrics

## Fundraising stance

Raise **£5–10m** into current agentic-commerce market once trigger is hit.

## Raise trigger

Open round when all are true:
- ≥3 stores with holdout-verified lift
- ≥5 paying pilots
- warm pipeline of ≥20 stores
- one live agency conversation

## Deck story

Answer platform risk head-on with:
- moat stack
- verified action-to-outcome dataset
- House Rules corpus
- cross-tool write depth
- Klaviyo precedent

Internal honesty:
- raise is substantially on story, pipeline and BFCM-verified proof
- MRR likely low tens of thousands
- do not confuse pitch with plan

## Operating model

Deliberately AI-native:
- tiny team
- high revenue per head
- founder salaries
- location-independent by design
- agents supporting engineering, sales, marketing, support, QA and product

A £5–10m round brings a board and growth expectations, so the operating model must be a stated strategy.

## Location

Toronto/Ottawa proximity has strategic weight:
- Shopify partner org
- Editions cycle
- acquisition optionality

New York is Shopify-adjacent, not Shopify.

## Guardrail metrics

### Daily-brief open rate

Target:
- >60%

Interpretation:
- habit and perceived value proxy

### Logo churn

Target:
- <3% per month

### CAC payback

Target:
- <3 months

Channel experiments report CAC per activated design partner.

### Approval quality

Target:
- ≥60% of proposed actions approved

Pair with:
- edit rate
- post-action satisfaction
- sub-5-second approvals flagged as rubber-stamp risk

### Cap compliance

Target:
- 100% of actions within caps
- zero House Rules violations

### Time to first verified win

Target:
- <30 days per merchant
