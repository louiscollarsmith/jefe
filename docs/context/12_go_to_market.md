# 12 — Go-to-Market

## Operating discipline

Breadth is allowed, but every channel gets:
- 4–6 week window
- one shared metric
- kill/scale decision on the date

Shared metric:
- CAC per activated design partner

Trying lots is a strategy.
Keeping losers alive is not.

## Channels

### Free AI teardown

Main funnel:

> Connect read-only, see the £X you are leaving on the table within 48 hours.

Built from COGS-free computables.

### Outbound

Apollo sequences to:
- ecom managers
- founders/owners A-cell

Target:
- £30k–£250k/month GMV
- StoreLeads/BuiltWith-style lists

Start design-partner outreach immediately. BFCM clock does not wait for build.

### LinkedIn

Founder voice plus AI-assisted content experiments.

No lift claims unless holdout-verified.

### SEO / AEO / AdWords

- capped paid spend
- AEO priority
- aim to be the answer AI assistants give for “AI ecom manager”

### AI-native sales ops

Claude-orchestrated sub-agents for:
- research
- list building
- follow-ups
- referrals
- reviews
- support

This doubles as marketing story:

> We run our company the way your store will be run.

### Case studies

Only holdout-verified numbers are case studies.

Everything else is a testimonial.

BFCM-verified numbers are the crown jewels.

### Design partners

Use Quiver-era relationships where legally permissible.

Check trade-sale non-solicit clauses before outreach.

### Agencies and community

- agency channel sequenced carefully
- ecom Twitter/X
- operator newsletters
- r/shopify
- App Store organic once listed

## Agency/auditor sequencing

Independent measurement and agency distribution can collide.

Do not court agencies while publicly exposing agency attribution claims.

Sequence:
1. Publicly audit ad platforms and native AI.
2. Offer agencies white-label proof tooling privately.
3. Open agency console in scale phase.
